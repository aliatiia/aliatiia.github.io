Ethereum:

The idea that you could wield software that summons disparate parties to autonomously congregate around an attractive/useful economic mechanism, in a rent-free (=max efficiency) and permissionless manner, is quite profound.

My interest is in building auto-pilot (governless) and rent-free public financial utilities. Currently Im working on a more robust version of this options market prototype, plus doing research into a Oracle-less purely ETH-backed stablecoin.

If you have a similar interest please get in touch!
